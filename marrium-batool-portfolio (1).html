01 Conversational AI / RAG
RAG Customer Support Chatbot
The Problem

Businesses receive a high volume of repetitive customer inquiries that demand consistent, accurate answers from their product docs, FAQs, and knowledge bases. Support teams spend hours on questions that could be automated, but generic chatbots return wrong or outdated answers.

The Solution

A Retrieval-Augmented Generation chatbot that ingests company knowledge bases, FAQs, and documentation into a vector database. When a customer asks a question, the agent retrieves the most relevant content and generates a precise, context-aware answer, significantly reducing support load while maintaining accuracy.

Tech Stack
Gemini RAG Pipeline Supabase Vector Search
02 Lead Generation / Scraping
Google Maps Lead Generation Agent
The Problem

Sales teams and agencies need targeted lists of local business owners with contact details, addresses, and business information. Manual research is painfully slow and inconsistent, and existing tools are either expensive or return low-quality data.

The Solution

An autonomous scraping agent that takes a business niche and location as input, then searches Google Maps and Google Search to extract structured business owner data including names, phone numbers, emails, and websites. The output is a clean, ready-to-use lead list, fully automated.

Tech Stack
Google Maps API Google Search API Data Enrichment Google Sheets
03 Lead Generation / Enrichment
LinkedIn Lead Generation and Data Enrichment Agent
The Problem

B2B companies need qualified LinkedIn leads filtered by industry, title, and company size. Manual prospecting is time-intensive and difficult to scale, and most scraping approaches are fragile without the right tooling.

The Solution

A lead generation and enrichment pipeline using Apify's LinkedIn scraper actors to extract prospect profiles at scale. The agent enriches each lead with additional professional context including job history and company info, delivering a sales-ready contact list with minimal manual effort.

Tech Stack
Apify LinkedIn Scraper Data Enrichment Google Sheets
04 Social Media / Brand Automation
Shadow Commenter Agent
The Problem

Brands want to build organic presence on Reddit by engaging in relevant discussions, but writing personalised, on-brand comments at scale is impossible manually. Generic auto-comments get flagged as spam and damage brand reputation.

The Solution

An agent that uses Apify to scrape relevant Reddit posts matching the brand's niche and keywords. For each post, an LLM generates a personalised comment tuned to the brand's voice and tone, sounding genuinely human. The Reddit App API then posts the comments automatically, creating authentic engagement at scale.

Tech Stack
Apify Reddit App API OpenAI GPT-4 Brand Voice Prompting
05 Voice AI / Lead Qualification
AI Voice Agent for Form Submission Lead Qualification
Built For

Sales teams and businesses that receive inbound form submissions and need to qualify leads immediately, before they go cold. This is for teams who lose deals simply because follow-up happens too late and qualification is inconsistent across reps.

What It Does

A voice AI agent that triggers the moment a form is submitted and calls the lead within seconds. It conducts a structured qualification conversation using natural speech, scores their responses, and routes hot leads to the sales team instantly so no opportunity is ever missed due to slow follow-up.

Tech Stack
VAPI Twilio Voice AI Webhooks Lead Routing
06 Voice AI / Scheduling
AI Voice Agent for Inquiries, Qualification and Appointment Booking
Built For

Businesses who are losing potential customers because calls come in after hours, during busy periods, or when no one is available to answer. This is for any business where missed calls mean missed revenue and manual booking creates unnecessary friction.

What It Does

A lifelike AI voice agent powered by Retell AI that handles inbound calls 24/7, answering customer inquiries naturally, qualifying prospects through conversation, and booking confirmed appointments directly into Google Calendar. It manages availability in real time, sends confirmations, and hands off to the human team when needed.

Tech Stack
Retell AI Google Calendar API Voice AI NLP Appointment Booking
